About Me

I went to study the subject at the University of California, Santa Cruz, and found a love of surfing, rock climbing, and triathlon training while there.

Hardware is my background by career, but a desire to learn more software is what led me to get involved with the UC Berkeley Coding Bootcamp. That led to creation of this portfolio page (which I've always wanted to have one!) and now I can track some of my personal projects.
